Part I - Information Collection

BESOR ASSOCIATES respects each individual's right to personal privacy. We will collect and use information through our Web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies solely to information collected at BESOR ASSOCIATES' Web site.
BESOR ASSOCIATES collects information through our Web site at several points.
We collect the following information about primary visitors: Name, Contact Details, Educational Records, Program Feedbacks. We may also collect Name, Contact Details, Educational Records, Program Feedbacks which our users divulge about other people, such as program sponsors and educational service providers.
BESOR ASSOCIATES does not actively market to children, and we never knowingly ask a child under 13 to divulge personal information. We collect the following general data that is not personally identifiable information: Educational Records, Program Feedbacks, Experience with programs. We collect demographic data in order to improve our service delivery.
We verify the accuracy of this information by contacting the stated parties involved. We collect this information through registration forms and feedback forms. The information collected are Name, Contact details, Educational Records, Program Feedbacks, Experience with programs. The information is collected voluntarily.
To receive our products and services, Name and Contact Details is mandatory because we will need this for request delivery. Other information we request is optional. We do not usually employ cookies.
A cookie is a small text file that our Web server may place on a user's computer hard drive to be a unique identifier. Cookies enable BESOR ASSOCIATES to track usage patterns and deliver customized content to users. Our cookies have an expiration date. Our cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.
